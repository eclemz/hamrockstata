Disposal Of Oil Waste

The monitoring center and supply plant keep track of the pipeline operational status around the
clock by the SCADA (Supervisory Control And Data Acquisition) system to support our capability
for safety respond to contingencies, such as remotely shutting down gas supply in the event of an
emergency.

Also, it installs equipment for ensuring safety in the event of an emergency: the emergency
shutdown valve (ESDV) in main valve stations and delivery points to clients for remotely shutting
down gas supply and the gas diffusion stack in main points of the pipeline network for air emission
of gas as necessary.

In addition, our staffs patrol the length of the pipeline, conduct maintenance and inspections of
facilities, and make security labels and signs with utmost care to ensure safe operation.
